Our Story

From a single ice cream parlor to the Philippines' beloved fast food chain

The Jollibee Journey

What started as an ice cream parlor in 1975 has grown into the Philippines' largest and most beloved fast food chain. Founded by Tony Tan Caktiong, Jollibee began with a simple dream: to serve great-tasting food that brings joy to Filipino families.

Our signature fried chicken, with its secret marinade and special coating, quickly became a household favorite. The sweet-style spaghetti, juicy burgers, and other Filipino-inspired dishes followed, each crafted to suit the unique taste preferences of Filipinos.

Today, Jollibee operates over 1,400 stores worldwide, but our heart remains in the Philippines, serving the communities that made us who we are today.
